Campus Ambassador Program

Campus Ambassador Program

About Campus Ambassador Program:

We at Spardha are looking for enthusiastic people to be an integral part of the organizing team of Spardha'19. We extend our welcome to all passionate individuals who would be working together with us and leading the contingents taking part in the festival from their respective colleges.

  What to gain?

Success is achieved by building up upon small blocks of trials. We provide you with an opportunity to expand your connection circles by interacting with people from diverse fields of various colleges across the country. It would be a test of your managerial and leadership skills by being a face of Spardha '19 in your college.


  Responsibilities

  1. PUBLICIZE - The CA would be expected to publicize Spardha'19 on all platforms, and means available.
  2. COORDINATE - The applicant would be working in hand with us to coordinate and lead their contingents.
  3. BRAINSTORM - The applicant would be working with the team to come up with fresh ideas together to make this edition of Spardha splendorous.

  Incentives

  1. Goodies and Prizes worth ₹ 15,000.
  2. Certificate of Recognition.
  3. Indelible opportunity to meet the esteemed guests.
  4. Exclusive Spardha merchandise and goodies.

  Eligibility

Applicants from all streams are welcome. Academic year is not a restriction, anyone who possess the zeal and dedication for Spardha'19 are eligible to apply.
